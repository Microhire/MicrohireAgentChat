# Westin Brisbane — MicroHire Comprehensive Venue Guide

MicroHire is the audio-visual (AV) partner for [The Westin Brisbane](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/bnewi-the-westin-brisbane/overview/), a luxury hotel in the heart of Brisbane, Australia. This guide details our collaboration: venue options (meeting rooms, conference halls, ballrooms, gala and reception spaces), capacities, room setup types, and the AV equipment and support we provide.

Every event space at The Westin Brisbane is supported by our professional team, on-site AV infrastructure, and the ability to scale up with advanced solutions such as LED walls, projection mapping, hybrid streaming, and staging.

## Room Setup Types

| Setup            | Description |
|------------------|-------------|
| **Boardroom Table** | Single large table with chairs around all sides. Ideal for meetings and discussions. |
| **U-Shape**      | Tables and chairs arranged in a U shape facing the presenter. Great for training and workshops. |
| **Cabaret**      | Round tables with chairs on two-thirds of the table facing the stage. Encourages group discussion. |
| **Classroom**    | Rows of tables and chairs facing the front. Best for seminars and note-taking sessions. |
| **Theatre**      | Rows of chairs facing the stage with no tables. Suited for presentations and large audiences. |
| **Banquet**      | Round tables with chairs all around. Ideal for meals, receptions, and social events. |
| **Hollow Square**| Tables arranged in a square with an open center. Ideal for discussions and team meetings. |
