Labour Product codes

AVTECH = Audio Visual Technician – Jack of all trades

AXTECH = Specialised Audio Technician

VXTECH = Specialised Vision Technician

LXTECH = Specialised Lighting Technician

 

Labour Task

SU = Setup

T&C = Test & Connect
Reh = Rehearsal

Op = Operate

PD = Pack Down



Once there is an operator the equipment inc laptops etc is assumed at the operator’s desk. We will need to ask the client if they want a laptop anything at stage.

Laptop @ stage = 2 x SDICROSS
These boxes extend the HDMI signal via a long SDI cable from the lectern to the operator’s desk so that they can switch between the laptops
